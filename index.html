About 'Me'

Hello! My name is Jimin Kim! Right now you're on STUDYRO2M, or what used to be my webpage!

It has been my dream for over a year to make my webpage a legitimate website that could be a proper business; well, here it is, thanks to all of the support from you!

Some of my practices from the very beginning of the webpage have been left behind, forming parts of the front page of this business. I did not expect it to happen. However, so many of you insisted on keeping them not only for their sentimental value (I hope not!) but also for a taste of personal touch that is so unique to this peculiar html/css/javascript learning website. I was hesitant at first, but gave it a go in that vote of confidence. Let me be honest, it looks good. So there you have it, and don't be embarrassed if I am!

And please again, 'take a look around, and I hope you'll enjoy your stay'!
